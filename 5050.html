50/50 Draw & Bar Tab

A 50/50 Draw is a pool of money where each dollar entered is considered a ticket for the entrant, where the winner receives half of the accumulated prize pool. For example: If you spend $100 in a prize pool of $1000, you have a 1 in 10 chance of winning $500. Kuah App is a Co-operative Not For Profit, so the other half is reinvested back into the community and pool of which it was created.
